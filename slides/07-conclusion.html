Conclusion

Oxidation and reduction reactions are the foundation of flow batteries. By understanding and controlling electron flow, we can utilize and store electrical energy safely and efficiently.

Why Flow Batteries Matter:

  • Long lifespan and flexibility
  • Excellent for large-scale energy storage
  • Perfect partner for renewable energy sources
