Factors Affecting Performance

🧪 Electrolyte Concentration

Higher concentration = more energy storage, but too high can cause unwanted reactions or precipitation

💨 Flow Rate

Optimal flow ensures efficient ion transport without excessive pumping energy

🌡️ System Temperature

Warmer temperatures speed reactions but too high can degrade components

⚡ Electrode Material

Advanced materials/designs improve efficiency and power output
