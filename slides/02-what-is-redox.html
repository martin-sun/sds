What is a Redox Reaction?

Redox = Oxidation-Reduction Reaction
A chemical reaction involving the transfer of electrons between two substances

Oxidation

⚛️ → ⚛️⁺ + e⁻

A substance loses electrons and its overall charge becomes more positive

Reduction

⚛️ + e⁻ → ⚛️⁻

A substance gains electrons and its overall charge becomes more negative

💡 Remember: Electrons can't just vanish — if one substance is giving up electrons, another must be receiving them. This electron flow creates electric current!
